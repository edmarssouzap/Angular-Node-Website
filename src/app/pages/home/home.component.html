This is Dream's Website

Dream is an American YouTuber who is known for his impressive abillities and height I.Q. decisions while playing Minecraft. Dream began his YouTube career in 2018 and gained popularity in 2019/2020 by uploading videos of his "Minecraft Manhunt" series and speedrun attempts. He also gained the former world record for speedrunning (beating the game as fast as possible) the 1.15 version of Minecraft.

Some of his Content

ManHunt

A game where one player tries to beat the Ender Dragon and win the game, while one or more players try to track him down and kill him.

Minecraft, but...

This is a series of videos where Dream code in minecraft files changing physics, mobs behavior, logic and more. The challenge is to beat the game, surviving the obstacles. Some of the changes may be an advantage, some will torment their lives.

SpeedRun

The challenge to beat the game as fast as possible, no rules, trying to get the World Record.
